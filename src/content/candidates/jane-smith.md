Jane Smith has served our community for over 15 years as a local business owner and education advocate. She is running for City Council to focus on sustainable development and improved public transportation.

## Platform

- Expand public transit options
- Support small businesses
- Invest in green infrastructure

## Experience

- Owner, Smith & Co. Hardware (2008-present)
- School Board Member (2015-2020)
- Downtown Business Association President (2018-2021)
